<p><strong>Celebrating Our SgIS Alumni Recipients</strong>
</p>
<p>We are proud to congratulate our Anderson Serangoon Junior College alumni
who have been awarded the prestigious Singapore-Industry Scholarship (SgIS).</p>
<p>SgIS offers a unique value proposition by fostering early and meaningful
engagement between scholars and their Sponsoring Organisations. Unlike
traditional scholarships that typically provide support only during the
course of study, SgIS establishes a long-term developmental pathway that
begins even before graduation. This strong alignment between academic development
and industry relevance ensures that, upon joining their organisations full-time,
scholars are already familiar with operations, aligned with organisational
missions, and well-prepared to contribute meaningfully.</p>
<p>From an industry perspective, SgIS plays a strategic role in building
a sustainable talent pipeline, strengthening competitiveness, and supporting
long-term growth. We commend our alumni for their dedication, passion,
and commitment, and we look forward to their continued impact in their
respective fields.</p>
