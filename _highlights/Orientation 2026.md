<h3><strong>Welcome 2026 JC1s!</strong></h3>
<p>Congratulations on your posting to Anderson Serangoon Junior College!
This is a new phase of your education journey, and we warmly welcome you
to the ASR family.</p>
<h4><strong>Reporting Time and Attire</strong></h4>
<p>The first day of school is on 4 February 2026, Wednesday. Please report
to our college at 7.55 a.m. in your secondary school uniform. Do inform
the General Office by calling 6459 6822 if you are unable to report to
college on 4 February 2026.</p>
