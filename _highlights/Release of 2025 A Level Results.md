<h2>Release of 2025 A-Level Results</h2>
<p>On <strong>27 February 2026</strong>, ASRJCians returned to the college
with anticipation and emotion to receive their <strong>2025 GCE A-Level results</strong>.
The atmosphere was filled with pride, gratitude and reflection as the college
community gathered to celebrate the achievements of the <strong>Class of 2025</strong>,
who completed their journey with grit, tenacity and resilience.</p>
<p>In his address to the cohort, <strong>Principal Mr Heng Yew Seng</strong> encouraged
graduates to cherish their memories in ASRJC, express gratitude to those
who had supported them, and continue to pay it forward to future generations.
He shared inspiring stories of students who embodied the college values
and lived out the vision of being <strong>Imaginative Thinkers and Caring Leaders</strong>.</p>
<p>Throughout their time in ASRJC, the cohort demonstrated perseverance as
they balanced academic pursuits with a wide range of co-curricular, leadership
and service learning experiences. Their journey reflects the spirit of
determination and growth that defines the ASRJC experience.</p>
<p>We would also like to thank our <strong>parents for their steadfast partnership</strong>,
and our <strong>teachers and non-teaching staff for their dedication and care</strong> in
guiding our students through these formative years.</p>
<p>As our graduates embark on new chapters—whether pursuing university studies,
fulfilling national service, or exploring other pathways—we are confident
that they will carry forward the friendships, values and lessons gained
during their time in ASRJC.</p>
<p>May they continue to hold close to their hearts our college motto:
<br><strong>Discere Servire – Non Mihi Solum</strong>  <em>(To Learn, To Serve – Not for Myself Alone).</em>
</p>
<p><strong>Congratulations to the Class of 2025. We are immensely proud of your achievements and wish you every success in the exciting journeys ahead.</strong>
</p>
