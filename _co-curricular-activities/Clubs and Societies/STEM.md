<p>Are you ready to explore the exciting world of Science, Technology, Engineering,
and Mathematics? Our STEM CCA is more than just a club – it’s a community
where curious minds come together to learn, create, and inspire each other.</p>
<p>Here, you’ll find a platform to dive deep into your STEM interests. Whether
it’s programming, experimenting with DNA extraction using fruits, or collaborating
with different stakeholders on interesting projects, we’ve got something
for everyone. Led by our passionate student leaders and guided by teacher
mentors, every session is an opportunity to learn and grow.</p>
<p>What makes our CCA special is the chance to put your skills to the test
in prestigious competitions. From the National Robotics Competition to
the Singapore Science and Engineering Fair and International Elementz Fair,
our members consistently shine through various STEM opportunities. These
experiences not only build your technical abilities but also teach resilience,
teamwork, and leadership.</p>
<p>So, why wait? Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and growth.
Together, let’s make STEM an unforgettable part of your junior college
experience!</p>
